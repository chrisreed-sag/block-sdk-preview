parseBinOpRHS

apama.analyticskit.framework.internal.parser.AST parseBinOpRHS(integer exprPrecedence, apama.analyticskit.framework.internal.parser.AST lhs)
Parse binary operator.

Parse a right hand side of a binary expression. Only parses tokens of equal or higher precedence that exprPrecedence. (e.g. 1*2 + 3 is all handled in one call, building AST(*, 1,2) and then AST(+,AST(*,1,2),3) .)

On finding an operator followed by a higher precedence operator, recurses - so 1 + 2 * 3 will recurse to generate AST(*,2,3) and then build AST(+,1,AST(*,2,3))
Parameters:
exprPrecedence
lhs
